about me.


My name is Nejc Ribič, a -year-old ambitious and versatile individual from Slovenia who approaches every endeavor with high standards. With a solid background in office technology and economics, I am motivated to bring new ideas to life and make a lasting impact in all that I do.

My work journey has taken me through roles in major firms, where I’ve gained invaluable experience in finance, customer service, and logistics. In finance, I developed practical skills in accounting and financial processes, becoming proficient in tools like SAP and Microsoft Office. Later, in customer service, I honed my abilities in managing client relationships and resolving inquiries, particularly in specialized fields like energy services. My role in logistics was especially formative, as I advanced to lead operations—overseeing delivery teams, optimizing fleet routes, and maintaining safety standards. These experiences taught me the art of efficiency and adaptability, demonstrating how teamwork and thoughtful leadership can drive success in fast-paced environments.

Academically, I hold a diploma in economics from a higher vocational college, providing a strong foundation to support my career ambitions. I am also multilingual, fluent in Slovenian, English, and Croatian, and currently improving my German, which opens doors for broader connections and collaboration. I believe each language enriches personal and professional interactions, offering fresh perspectives and a wider worldview.

Looking to the future, I envision myself contributing to sustainable progress, where innovation and environmental awareness are at the forefront of change. I am passionate about fostering collective growth through impactful work, helping build a future where both individuals and communities thrive.
