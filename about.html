about me.


I am deeply committed to building a future that embodies my personal ethos, encapsulated by the phrase "The architecture of my own future". My life is driven by a relentless pursuit of passion, motivation, and joy. As an individual with diverse interests and hobbies, I find fulfillment in exploring the realms of fashion, cars, technology, finances, investments, watches, architectural design, digital media manipulation, music, programming, website building, and aviation. My creative approach is rooted in the principles of minimalism, and I constantly seek to apply modern thinking and innovative problem-solving methodologies to all of my pursuits.

My name is Nejc Ribič, and I am a 21-year-old individual from Slovenia who is fluent in Slovenian, Croatian, and English. Additionally, I am currently studying German to expand my linguistic abilities. As an avid learner, I do not believe in giving up and always strive for growth. I am currently pursuing a degree in economics at a college and hope to hone my skills further to work in a challenging environment with new opportunities to provide quality service for individuals and organizations worldwide.

During my academic journey under the guidance of my senior high school and vocational college, I have completed several practical trainings both in my home country and abroad. These trainings have enabled me to broaden my knowledge and expertise in various areas, including business, teamwork, time management, problem-solving, English communication, and the usage of digital tools such as Microsoft Office 365, Adobe tools, and other Office-driven applications. Through these experiences, I have been able to stay abreast of technological innovations, and they have provided me with valuable insights and skills that I can apply to future endeavors.

In the future, I envision myself living in a thriving metropolitan area such as Ljubljana, the capital of my country, or any other significant urban center that presents new and exciting social and professional prospects. These cities offer vast employment opportunities and provide an excellent platform for creating new friendships and expanding social networks.
