about me.


"The architecture of your own future" is a quote that resonates with me, one could say I do my best to live by it since having a life filled with passion, motivation and joy is something I truly strive for. My hobbies are mostly related to fashion, cars, technology, watches, interior & exterior design, digital photo & video manipulation, music, programming, building websites, and even aviation! I'm also a big fan of minimalism and love to create with the idea "less is more".

My name is Nejc Ribič, I am 21 years old coming from Slovenia, I am fluent in Slovenian, Croatian, and English languages while also learning French. I am a continuous learner and I do not like taking "I cannot" as an option. As a matter of fact, I am currently visiting college, where I am studying economics. Once I finish my professional education I hope to further develop and master my skills and work in an environment filled with new challenges and opportunities to help our world and provide quality service for people and organizations all over the world.

I have been developing my past knowledge through my hobbies on specific apps which I think are sufficient to know to help one be more tech-savvy and keep up with the neverending technology innovations, such as Adobe, Microsoft, and other programming programs.

As for the future, I really see myself within a bigger city like one in my country named Ljubljana, or if the opportunity arises even any other bigger city metropolis since such usually does not fail to provide great work opportunities and the creation of new social wires and friendship bonds.
