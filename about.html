about me.


Living with passion is what I strictly adhere to. “Always do what you love and only then you will truly live” is the quote I live by, as it really motivates me to do things I find joy in. My hobbies are related to aviation, creating & designing digital pictures, music, editing, gaming, fashion, and programming. I also love minimalism and believe in the saying "less is more" and I also try to implement minimalism into my work.

My name is Nejc Ribič, currently 18 years old living in Slovenia. I speak Slovenian, Croatian, English, and I am to learn French in the future. I am a continuous learner, where 'I cannot' is not an option. I am currently finishing my high school education in Slovenia in ŠCKS, where I am studying as a Computer Technician, and from there, I plan to go to the College to study informatics and business.

Languages I work with: HTML, CSS, JS, JAVA. Programs I use: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe premiere Visual Studio Code, NetBeans.

My long-term plan is to live either in Ljubljana or later on in Paris or any other big European leading city full of possibilities and resources and to get a position as a UI, UX designer, or position as a data analytic. In the future, I would also love to play apart in technology or fashion-oriented business.
