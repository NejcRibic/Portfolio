about me.


"Always do what you love and only then will you truly live" is a quote that really resonates with me, you could say I do my best to live by it since having a life filled with passion, motivation and joy is something I truly strive for. My hobbies are mostly related to aviation, digital art, music, editing, gaming, fashion, and programming. I'm also a big fan of minimalism, I love creating with the idea of "less is more".

My name is Nejc Ribič, I'm 18 years old and I live in Slovenia, I'm fluent in Slovenian, Croatian and English and I'm learning French. I'm a continuous learner and I don't like taking "I cannot" as an option. I'm still in high school, as a matter of fact, I'm just finishing my fourth and final year at Šolski Center Krško-Sevnica as a Computer Technician, after that, I plan on going to college and getting a degree in Business and Informational Technology.

I work with HTML, CSS, JS, and JAVA, software I use includes Adobe PS, Adobe Premiere Visual Studio Code, and NetBeans.

I plan on moving to Ljubljana and working from there, or better yet, Paris or any other larger European city, because of the possibilities and resources that these places offer compared to Slovenia. I want to work as a UI/UX designer, or as a data analytic, I'm also trying to find ways of how I can implement my knowledge in these fields to a fashion-oriented business or any of my other hobbies.
