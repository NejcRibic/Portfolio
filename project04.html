The following gallery of digital art represents pieces of my work that I have made in the past, using Adobe Premiere Photoshop. Being very passionate about designing and creating digital art, makes hours feel like minutes and finishing products so much more rewarding. The knowledge I gain from this hobby of mine would in best hope and interest be further advanced and implemented into professional waters, where I could express what I am good at and combine that with other hobbies of mine.

PROJECT 01


BOARDING PASS PHONE CASE






I have started with the idea of making a DIY (Do It Yourself) phone case, where I got inspired by a few of the makers, where they took the idea of a clear phone case and plane ticket's boarding pass and merged it together, creating rather pleasing phone case, resulting in the end-result shown in the picture above.


I took a custom approach, where with the help of the Adobe program, I managed to re-create such design, and create custom QR and UPC-A code. I have also personalized my own text, which has personal meaning, alongside the custom flight numbers, while also improving the overall image's graphics, resulting in the sharper print-ready digital image.


Printed this high-quality self-made digital boarding pass resulted in the successful ending, shown in the following picture of the end result.


CONCLUSION


TIME SPENT : UP TO THREE DAYS


By viewing the fantastic product I liked and instead of buying into the product I managed to take another approach of further brainstorming and exploring the so-much-to-explore surface, I managed to create a custom phone case that has its own 'easter-eggs' and reminds me back of the days when Covid-19 wasn't a thing and flying with airlines was so much more doable.
